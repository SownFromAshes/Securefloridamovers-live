Frequently Asked Questions

Get expert answers to common moving questions from our professional Tampa Bay movers.

General Moving Questions

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (May-September). However, we can often accommodate last-minute moves based on our availability. The more notice you can give, the better!

Are you a licensed and insured moving company?

Absolutely. Secure Florida Movers is fully licensed by the Florida Department of Transportation (FL License #IM2024) and carries comprehensive liability and cargo insurance for your protection and peace of mind.

Pricing and Payment

How are moving costs calculated?

Local moves (typically under 50 miles) are charged by the hour. Long-distance moves are based on the weight of your shipment and the distance to your destination. We provide clear, upfront estimates with no hidden fees.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes. For the most accurate price, we recommend a free in-home or virtual estimate where our consultant can assess the scope of your move.

Packing and Preparation

Do you offer packing services?

We do! We offer a full range of packing services, from full-service packing of your entire home to partial packing for fragile items like kitchenware or artwork. We use professional-grade materials to ensure everything is secure.

What items can you not transport?

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot transport hazardous materials (e.g., propane tanks, paint, chemicals), perishable food items, live plants on long-distance moves, or personal items of extraordinary value like jewelry and important documents, which you should transport personally.
