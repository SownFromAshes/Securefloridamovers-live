Local Moving FAQs

Answers to common questions about moving within the Tampa Bay area.

What is considered a 'local move'?

Typically, a local move is any relocation within a 50-mile radius. This covers moves between Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, and other surrounding communities in the Tampa Bay metropolitan area.

How is the cost of a local move calculated?

Local moves are usually priced on an hourly basis. The rate depends on the number of movers and trucks required for your job. The final cost is determined by the total time it takes to complete your move, from the moment our crew arrives until the last item is placed in your new home.

Do you offer packing services for local moves?

Absolutely! We offer flexible packing solutions to fit your needs. We can provide full-service packing for your entire home, partial packing for specific rooms like the kitchen, or simply provide you with high-quality packing materials if you prefer to pack yourself.

Are my belongings insured during a local move?

Yes. As a licensed moving company, we provide basic liability coverage as required by law. We also offer more comprehensive valuation coverage options for added protection and peace of mind. We can discuss these options with you during the quoting process.
